About

A Student of Law and Literature working on the relationship between justice and poetics.

I am an undergraduate student both at Universidad Autonoma de Madrid and UNED. I consider Literature to be not only a reflection of society but also a means of justice. I have worked on topics such as censorship trials under Napoleon III's rule or the portrayal of terrorism in postconflict novels from the Basque Country and Northern Ireland during my bachelor's. I have also been a volunteer in Uganda, class representative and member of the organisational comitee of the IX Philobiblion Congress. I am currently applying to Postgraduate programs where I can keep developing my research skills and social awareness.

  • Languages: Spanish, English, French, Portuguese, Latin
  • Skills: Text editing, Literary Commentary,
  • Technological Skills: Microsoft Office, CORSE online editor for phonetical and phonological transcriptions
